# Outbound Data & Third-Party Sharing

Last updated: 1 Dec 2025

Tonight? shares limited information with third-party providers to operate and improve the service. We disclose these integrations here for transparency.

| Provider | Purpose | Data shared |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) | Measure app usage trends and diagnose crashes | App usage events, device information, installation identifiers (non-identifiable) |
| Google AdMob (Google LLC) | Deliver ads and measure performance | Advertising identifier, coarse interaction metrics |

We only transmit the minimum data required, and never sell personal information. You can review additional details in the Privacy Policy and disable analytics or ads from your device settings where supported.
